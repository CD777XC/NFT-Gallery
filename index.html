Trust:

The NFT & crypto world requires all your attention when processing to a purchase, an exchange, an investment, a staking and all the other possibilities that Web 3.0 offers to us.
Since I started investing in crypto's and NFT's I had the opportunity (or bad luck) to experience some of the issues and scams this world could leads you to. I will then make a short intro to make sure you don't repeat those errors. It is better to learn from the others than by your own mistakes.
NFT's project have discord servers where you can check the updates, announcement and sneak-peeks. You should never join discord another way than:

  • Through a trusted friend invite
  • The discord link on the official project website
  • The discord link on the official marketplace project page
